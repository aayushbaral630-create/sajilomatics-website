About

Five people. One obsession: effortless.

Sajilomatics was founded in Kathmandu in 2026 on a simple irritation: watching brilliant businesses lose their best hours to work a machine should be doing.

The story

Born from the word for “easy.”

In Nepali, sajilo means easy, effortless. It's what every client says after the system goes live — and it's the standard we hold every line of code to.

We started as a digital marketing consultant and a senior software engineer comparing notes on the same frustration from opposite sides: marketers drowning in manual campaign work, and businesses buying software that didn't fit them. The answer was obvious — build the automation and the software together, under one roof.

Today we're a five-person studio in Kathmandu shipping systems that run recruitment agencies, hotels, and marketing operations. Small by design: every client works directly with the people building their system.
